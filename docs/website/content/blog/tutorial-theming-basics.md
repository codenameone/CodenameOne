Theming allows us to adapt the look of an application in a similar way to CSS while styling allows us to do so within the code. In this tutorial I cover only a small fraction of the subject matter but after going thru it you should have a decent understanding of the forces at play and the tools you can use.

I didn’t cover many crucial subjects such as borders (9-piece or otherwise), backgrounds, [in place style editing](/blog/edit-styles-simulator.html) or many other things. All of these things are worth knowing and understanding and hopefully will be covered in a future video individually.
